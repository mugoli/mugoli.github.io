Mugoli Samba is a journalist and freelance writer based in Toronto. She graduated with a combined honours degree in journalism and African studies from Carleton University. She worked as an assistant digital editor with the UC Observer Magazine, a newsroom intern with the Windsor Star, and spent ten days sailing across the St. Lawrence River as a social media reporter for Radio-Canada Québec.
