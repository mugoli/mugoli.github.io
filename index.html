I’m a Toronto-based news editor at Apple News, where I help curate the best of local, national, and international journalism for millions of Canadian readers. I also oversee the curation of three weekly newsletters, and lead the editorial programming for major cultural moments.

Prior to joining Apple, I worked as a broadcast and print reporter for The Globe and Mail, CBC/Radio-Canada, and more. My magazine feature writing has earned a Canadian Church Press Award, and an honourable mention at the National Magazine Awards.

I’m curious about the ways technology, information, equality, and human life intersect. I want to leverage all of my skills to nurture hope amid the compounding crises of our times.
