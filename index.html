Mugoli Samba
I’m a Toronto-based journalist and news editor.

I currently work at Apple News, where I help curate the best of local, national, and international journalism for millions of Canadian readers. I also oversee the curation of three weekly newsletters, and lead the editorial programming for major cultural moments.

Prior to joining Apple, I worked as a broadcast and print reporter for The Globe and Mail, CBC/Radio-Canada, and more. My magazine feature writing has earned a Canadian Church Press Award, and an honourable mention at the National Magazine Awards. I'm also a former National Magazine Awards and Digital Publishing Awards juror.

I’m curious about the ways technology, information, equality, and human life intersect. Looking ahead, I want to leverage all of my skills and experience to nurture hope amid the compounding crises of our times.

When I'm not working, I'm at the park, at a show, or living inside of this painting.
